# Canonical Decision

## Selected Source

- `01-manuscript/ninety-nine-canonical-source.doc`

## Basis For Selection

- It is by far the largest and most complete manuscript file in the source directory.
- Its metadata identifies it directly as `Ninety-Nine`.
- It contains roughly 64.8k words and 206 pages, making it the only evident full manuscript candidate.
- It was last saved on December 29, 2008, which is later than the fragment and cut files found nearby.

## Caution

Canonical here means `best available source`, not `complete` or
`publication-ready`.

This manuscript is being promoted as the live source for the workspace
while preserving the known fact that the novella is incomplete and
missing at least one major ending section.
